# xeonio_linux
The xenio_linux project aims to meet the needs of musicians and artists who align with free software, more specifically the Debian philosophy. The distro fully aligns with the commitment to stability and longevity of Debian Stable, but severely facilitates various resources for artists and musicians who depend on free solutions, in addition to, of course, including the kernel, Mesa, Nvidia drivers (my personal need), as well as pre-configured multimedia and design plugins and software.
